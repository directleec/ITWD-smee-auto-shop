Tires

Smee's Auto Shop offers a wide range of new and used tires, as well as tire rotation and balance services, to help keep your vehicle running smoothly and safely on the road.

Smog

At Smee's Auto Shop, we are a STAR-certified smog check station, providing reliable and efficient smog certification services to help you register your vehicle with the DMV and ensure compliance with state emissions regulations.

Our Services

  • Maintenance services: From routine oil changes and tire rotations to brake inspections and engine tune-ups, we offer a variety of maintenance services to keep your vehicle in top condition.
  • Transmission repair: Our experienced technicians use the latest equipment and techniques to diagnose and repair transmission issues quickly and efficiently.
  • Check engine diagnostics: We use state-of-the-art diagnostic equipment to identify and repair issues related to your vehicle's check engine light.
  • Smog certification: We are a STAR-certified smog check station and can help you obtain the necessary certification to register your vehicle with the DMV.
  • Tire services: We offer a variety of tire services, including rotation, balance, and sales of new and used tires.
  • Brake services: We provide comprehensive brake services, including inspections, repairs, and replacements.
  • Electrical system services: Our technicians can diagnose and repair issues related to your vehicle's electrical system, including battery replacement and alternator repair.
